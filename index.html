The first step to starting a family...

For years, we have strived to keep our animals in save arms. Our goal is to provide good care to our pets, and give them away to deserving and loving people. We treat all of our animals (and customers) right.

We were founded in 1986, and have helped countless animals find homes after being abandoned, or even brutally treated. Our name is immensely respected due to the years of training and love put into our pets.
